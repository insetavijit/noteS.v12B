# **How to Approach : Page Builder OR Gutenberg Configuration**

> **Senior Developer Guidance:**  
> Choosing between **Gutenberg (Block Editor)** and a **Page Builder** (Elementor, Bricks, Divi, Breakdance) must be based on **performance, scalability, project requirements, and client capability**, not popularity.  
> Gutenberg is the future of WordPress core â€” fast, lightweight, native, and deeply integrated.  
> Page builders offer faster visual creation, advanced design flexibility, and complex layouts with less custom code.

Professional developers evaluate **goals, performance budgets, content management needs, and handover expectations**.  
The best choice is the one that aligns with purpose â€” not the trend.
