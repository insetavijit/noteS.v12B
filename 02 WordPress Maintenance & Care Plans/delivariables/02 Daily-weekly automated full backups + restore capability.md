# **How to Approach : Automated Backup Strategy & Restore Testing**

> **Senior Developer Guidance:**  
> Backups are the **last line of defense** for any digital system.  
> If the website is hacked, an update breaks functionality, hosting fails, or a human error occurs ‚Äî **backup + restore capability is the only guarantee of survival**.

A professional backup system is not ‚Äúdownload and store files manually‚Äù; it is an **automated scheduled strategy** with tested **restore workflows** to ensure recovery in minutes, not days.

Backups are useless **until they are restore-tested**.
