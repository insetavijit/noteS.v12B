# **How to Approach : Broken Links Scan & Fix**

> **Senior Developer Guidance:**  
> Broken links damage user experience, reduce search engine trust, increase bounce rate, and hurt brand credibility.  
> They often appear due to deleted pages, changed URLs, migrated content, incorrect internal linking, external resource removal, or plugin/theme replacements.  
> Professional maintenance includes **regular scanning, fixing, redirection strategy, and validation**.

A good website should never lead users to dead ends ‚Äî every path must be intentional.

---

## üîç **Broken Link Monitoring Workflow**

|Stage|Action|
|---|---|
|**1. Automated link scanning**|Scheduled crawl for broken links|
|**2. Identify internal & external issues**|404, 410, 500 response errors|
|**3. Map link sources & destinations**|Locate where links are used|
|**4. Fix internal link paths**|Correct page or resource mapping|
|**5. Set 301 redirects where needed**|Preserve SEO value & user flow|
|**6. Remove or replace dead external links**|Ensure content integrity|
|**7. Retest after fixes**|Verify complete resolution|
