# **How to Approach : GloriaFood (Restaurant Online Ordering & Table Reservation System)**

> **Senior Developer Guidance:**  
> **GloriaFood** is an **all-in-one online food ordering & restaurant reservation platform** that integrates with WordPress via a dedicated plugin.  
> Unlike Food Store (which is WooCommerce-based), GloriaFood is a **standalone ordering engine** providing **ordering, delivery, table reservations, POS integration, and real-time restaurant management** with a very modern UI and mobile app support.

Use GloriaFood when a restaurant needs a **free, fast, ready-to-use ordering system with table management**, without the complexity of WooCommerce.

### **Features Overview**

|**Feature**|**Brief Description**|
|---|---|
|**Full Online Food Ordering System**|Display menu, take orders, manage delivery & pickup.|
|**Table Reservation System Built-in**|Customer can choose date, time & number of people.|
|**Restaurant Mobile Management App**|Receive, confirm & manage orders on mobile/tablet.|
|**Advanced Menu Builder**|Create menu categories, variations, extras, and add-ons.|
|**Delivery Zone Management**|Create delivery maps with fee control & order validation.|
|**Real-time Order Notifications**|App & dashboard alerts for new orders and updates.|
|**Multi-location Support**|Manage multiple restaurants from one account.|
|**Payment Integration**|Online card payments available with add-ons; supports cash on delivery.|
|**Promo Codes & Marketing Tools**|Discount coupons, free delivery, marketing automation.|
|**POS & Printer Integration (Add-ons)**|Compatible with hardware receipt printers & restaurant POS.|
