# **How to Approach : Razorpay (Online Payment Gateway for India)**

> **Senior Developer Guidance:**  
> **Razorpay** is one of the most widely used **Indian payment gateways** that supports **UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, wallets, EMI, and subscription billing**.  
> It integrates seamlessly with **WooCommerce, booking systems, LMS platforms, membership sites, and donation forms**, offering **fast checkout, great UI, and reliable fraud protection**.

Use Razorpay when building **payment-enabled Indian business websites**, especially where **UPI-based conversions**, **subscription billing**, or **checkout optimization** are important.
