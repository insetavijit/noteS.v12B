# **How to Approach : MemberPress (Membership & Subscription Platform)**

> **Senior Developer Guidance:**  
> **MemberPress** is an **enterprise-grade membership and paywall system** for WordPress that enables the creation of **subscription-based websites, protected content areas, and course-based communities**.  
> It provides powerful tools for **billing automation, access control, pricing pages, LMS capabilities, and integration with e-commerce and learning platforms**.

Use MemberPress when building **premium membership communities**, **subscription programs**, or **recurring revenue models**, especially where **advanced control and scalability** are required.

### **Features Overview**

|**Feature**|**Brief Description**|
|---|---|
|**Membership Levels & Access Rules**|Restrict content by page, post, category, tag, courses, or custom rules.|
|**Recurring Subscriptions**|Automated renewals & billing with Stripe, PayPal, Authorize.Net.|
|**Built-in Course Builder (MemberPress Courses)**|Drag-and-drop LMS system for lesson creation & student tracking.|
|**Powerful Paywall Control**|Lock content with dynamic access and drip controls.|
|**Coupons & Pricing Pages**|Beautiful pricing tables & promotional discount systems.|
|**Automated Email Notifications**|Welcome, renewal, reminder & cancellation messaging.|
|**Reports & Revenue Analytics**|Track member performance, sales, churn & activity.|
|**Content Drip Scheduling**|Release lessons or content over time.|
|**Integrations & Automation**|Works with WooCommerce, BuddyBoss, Zapier, CRM tools and email marketing platforms.|
|**Group & Corporate Access**|Sell multiple seats for teams, schools & organizations.|

### **Ideal Project Types**
