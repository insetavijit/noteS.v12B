# **How to Approach : Stripe (Global Online Payment Gateway)**

> **Senior Developer Guidance:**  
> **Stripe** is a leading **international payment gateway** designed for global online businesses, supporting **credit/debit cards, wallets, bank transfers, Apple Pay, Google Pay, subscriptions, invoices, and international currency payments**.  
> It integrates seamlessly with **WooCommerce, booking systems, LMS platforms, membership websites, donation systems, and SaaS billing models**.

Use Stripe when projects require **international payments**, **multi-currency support**, **subscription billing automation**, or **global customer reach**.
### **Features Overview**

|**Feature**|**Brief Description**|
|---|---|
|**Card, Wallet & Bank Payments**|Supports Visa, MasterCard, Amex, Apple Pay, Google Pay & more.|
|**Multi-Currency Support**|Accept payments worldwide in 135+ currencies.|
|**Subscription & Recurring Billing**|Perfect for memberships, SaaS, coaching plans & LMS.|
|**WooCommerce & Platform Integration**|Easy setup for product checkout & cart payments.|
|**Payment Links & Checkout Pages**|Create hosted checkout pages without coding.|
|**Invoices & Tax Handling**|Automatic invoice generation & tax calculation.|
|**Dispute & Refund Management**|Full dispute resolution dashboard.|
|**Fraud Detection (Radar)**|AI-driven risk control for secure transactions.|
|**Marketplace & Split Payments**|Stripe Connect for multi-vendor systems.|
|**Developer & API Friendly**|Build custom payment flows with APIs & webhooks.|
