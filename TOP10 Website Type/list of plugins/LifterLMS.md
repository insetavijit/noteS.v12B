# **How to Approach : LifterLMS (Learning Management System)**

> **Senior Developer Guidance:**  
> **LifterLMS** is a **highly flexible and feature-rich LMS plugin** that enables the creation of **online learning platforms**, combining courses, memberships, and coaching functionality in one system.  
> It is ideal for projects requiring **powerful membership access control**, **coaching-style programs**, and **community-driven learning** with built-in engagement tools.

Use LifterLMS when you need **course + membership + coaching + payment bundling** in a single unified platform.
### **Features Overview**

|**Feature**|**Brief Description**|
|---|---|
|**Drag-and-Drop Course Builder**|Create structured courses with lessons, modules & multimedia.|
|**Native Membership System**|Protect content, restrict access & sell membership packages.|
|**Payment Integration**|Stripe, PayPal, WooCommerce & recurring subscription billing.|
|**Assignments & Quizzes**|Reviewable submissions, timed quizzes, grading & scoring.|
|**Engagement Automation**|Trigger emails, achievements, badges & certificates.|
|**Student Dashboard & Profiles**|Track progress, achievements & enrolled courses.|
|**Coaching & Private Group Learning**|Offer 1-on-1 coaching or team-based learning experiences.|
|**Course Bundles & Pricing Models**|Bundled programs, upsells, coupons, multi-tier pricing.|
|**CRM & Automation Integrations**|Integrates with Zapier, Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, etc.|
