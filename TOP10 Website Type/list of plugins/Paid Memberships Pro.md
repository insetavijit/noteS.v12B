# **How to Approach : Paid Memberships Pro (Membership Management System)**

> **Senior Developer Guidance:**  
> **Paid Memberships Pro (PMP)** is a **powerful membership and subscription management plugin** for WordPress, used to build **access-controlled content, subscription billing systems, and member communities**.  
> It is designed for businesses that require **role-based content restriction**, **recurring payments**, **membership levels**, and **advanced billing automation**.

Use Paid Memberships Pro when a platform needs **tiered access**, **member-only content**, **automated renewal payments**, or **integration with LMS / community / CRM systems**.

### **Features Overview**

|**Feature**|**Brief Description**|
|---|---|
|**Membership Level Management**|Create unlimited plans with different pricing and access rules.|
|**Content Restriction System**|Protect pages, posts, categories, videos, courses & downloads.|
|**Recurring Subscription Billing**|Automatic renewals via Stripe, PayPal, RazorPay, Authorize.Net etc.|
|**Checkout & Registration Forms**|Customizable front-end sign-up & payment flows.|
|**Discount Codes & Promotions**|Offer coupons, special pricing & seasonal deals.|
|**Member Dashboard & Profile**|Users can manage subscriptions and billing details.|
|**Email Notifications & Automation**|Renewal reminders, welcome emails, expiry notices.|
|**Reports & Revenue Analytics**|Track member signups, cancellations & income growth.|
|**Integrations & Add-ons Library**|80+ add-ons (LMS, WooCommerce, BuddyBoss, Zapier, CRMs).|
|**Community & LMS Compatibility**|Works well with BuddyPress, BuddyBoss, TutorLMS & LearnDash.|
