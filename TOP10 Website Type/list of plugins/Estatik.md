# **How to Approach : Estatik (Real Estate Listing & Property Management Plugin)**

> **Senior Developer Guidance:**  
> **Estatik** is a **real estate listing and property management plugin** designed for creating **property directory, agent listing, and real estate marketplace websites**.  
> It provides essential tools like **property search filters, map integration, agents dashboard, media galleries, and IDX/MLS support** (Pro), making it ideal for real estate agencies and rental platforms.

Use Estatik when building **property listing websites**, **broker or agency platforms**, or **rent/sale directories** requiring **filtering, location search, and structured real estate data**.

### **Features Overview**

|**Feature**|**Brief Description**|
|---|---|
|**Listing Management System**|Add and manage property listings with full details.|
|**Advanced Search & Filters**|Search by type, area, price, rooms, location, and more.|
|**Google Maps & Mapbox Integration**|Display properties on interactive maps.|
|**Photo & Video Gallery**|High-quality media support including sliders and floor plans.|
|**Agents & Agencies Profiles**|Create agent dashboards with independent listings.|
|**Front-End Submission**|Let agents add properties from the frontend.|
|**Mortgage Calculator**|Built-in EMI/loan calculation for buyer interest.|
|**Property Wishlist & Compare**|Improve user decision-making & experience.|
|**IDX/MLS Integration (Pro)**|Sync listings directly from MLS platforms.|
|**Multilingual & Multi-Currency Support**|Global real-estate compatibility.|
