# **How to Approach : Houzez (Real Estate Theme + Property System)**

> **Senior Developer Guidance:**  
> **Houzez** is a **premium real estate WordPress theme with a built-in property management system**, designed specifically for creating **real estate agency websites, property directories, and marketplace platforms**.  
> Unlike standalone plugins, Houzez includes **UI components, search filtering, mapping tools, agent dashboards, CRM-style workflows, and monetization features**, making it an all-in-one solution for real estate businesses.

Use Houzez when you need a **ready-to-launch real estate design with professional layouts, advanced search filters, property submission features, and strong integration with maps and CRM workflows**.

---

### **Features Overview**

|**Feature**|**Brief Description**|
|---|---|
|**Property Listing Builder**|Create detailed property listings with custom fields & flexible layout modules.|
|**Advanced Search & Filters**|Multi-criteria search: location, price, rooms, features, property type etc.|
|**Google Maps / OpenStreetMap / Mapbox**|Map search with clusters, pins, and geolocation filters.|
|**Front-End Property Submission**|Agents & users can add/edit listings from frontend dashboards.|
|**Membership & Monetization System**|Sell packages, featured listing spots & paid agent profiles.|
|**Built-In CRM Tools**|Lead management, inquiries tracking, saved searches & alerts.|
|**Agent & Agency Profiles**|Manage profiles, reviews, team roles & contact details.|
|**Property Compare & Favorites**|User experience tools for property decision-making.|
|**IDX/MLS Integration Support**|Sync MLS listings via third-party providers (with add-ons).|
|**Ready Design Templates**|Dozens of premium real estate page designs & blocks.|
