# **How to Approach : ShortPixel (Image Optimization Plugin)**

> **Senior Developer Guidance:**  
> **ShortPixel** is an **advanced image optimization and compression plugin** focused on maximizing performance through **aggressive compression, WebP/AVIF conversion, and automated image delivery**.  
> It is ideal for sites with **large volumes of high-resolution images** or where **maximum reduction in file size** is critical for performance and bandwidth savings.

Use ShortPixel when you need **deeper compression control**, **image CDN support**, and **automatic next-gen formats** for top Core Web Vital scores.

### **Features Overview**

|**Feature**|**Brief Description**|
|---|---|
|**Advanced Image Compression**|Lossy, Glossy, and Lossless compression modes.|
|**WebP & AVIF Conversion**|Converts images to next-gen formats for massive performance gains.|
|**ShortPixel CDN (Optimized Delivery)**|Delivers resized and optimized images through a global CDN.|
|**Bulk Optimization Tool**|Optimize thousands of images at once.|
|**Smart Auto-Resize & Scaling**|Automatically resizes images based on container dimension.|
|**Optimize All Media Files**|Works with thumbnails, retina versions, and external folders.|
|**PDF Optimization**|Compress PDF documents for download sections or resource pages.|
|**Backup Option**|Stores originals for rollback or quality adjustments.|
|**API & Developer Support**|Custom optimization workflow for advanced builds.|
|**WooCommerce & Gallery Compatible**|Best for image-heavy shops and galleries.|
