# **How to Approach : WooCommerce**

> **Senior Developer Guidance:**  
> **WooCommerce** is the **official e-commerce framework for WordPress**, enabling full online store functionality including product management, checkout, payments, shipping, and inventory.  
> It is highly customizable, scalable, and supported by thousands of extensions â€” ideal for building stores ranging from small shops to enterprise retail systems.

Use WooCommerce when **selling physical or digital products**, **subscriptions**, **bookings**, or **services**, and when the project requires **flexibility, ownership, and custom workflow control**.

---

### **Features Overview**

|**Feature**|**Brief Description**|
|---|---|
|**Product Management System**|Add physical, digital, variable, and grouped products.|
|**Secure Checkout System**|Fully customizable checkout & cart pages.|
|**Payment Gateway Support**|Works with Stripe, RazorPay, PayPal, Cash on Delivery & 100+ gateways.|
|**Inventory & Stock Control**|Track product quantities, backorders, and SKU organization.|
|**Shipping & Tax Rules**|Zone-based shipping, weight-based pricing, automated tax.|
|**WooCommerce Extensions Library**|Add subscriptions, bookings, marketplace systems, POS, etc.|
|**Coupon & Discount System**|Promotions, offers, cart discounts & referral programs.|
|**Order & Customer Management**|Dashboard for invoices, status tracking, refunds & fulfillment.|
|**Analytics & Reports**|Revenue dashboards, sales insights & performance metrics.|
|**Theme & Builder Compatibility**|Full integration with Elementor, Gutenberg, Bricks & page templates.|

---

### **Ideal Project Types**

- Online stores selling physical products
    
- Digital product shops (PDF, courses, software)
    
- Subscription & membership stores
    
- Booking-based businesses (salon, clinic, coaching)
    
- Restaurants with online ordering
    
- Multi-vendor marketplaces (like Amazon/Etsy style)
    
- Print-on-demand & dropshipping stores
