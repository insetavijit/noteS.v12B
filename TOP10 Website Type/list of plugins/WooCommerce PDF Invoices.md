# **How to Approach : WooCommerce PDF Invoices**

> **Senior Developer Guidance:**  
> **WooCommerce PDF Invoices** is a plugin that automatically **generates, attaches, and sends PDF invoices** for WooCommerce orders.  
> It improves **order documentation, customer experience, tax compliance, and business workflow automation**, making it essential for stores that require **billing records, GST/Tax invoicing, and downloadable receipts**.

Use WooCommerce PDF Invoices when the store needs **professional invoice documents**, **automated email attachment**, **download links**, and **admin order processing efficiency**.

---

### **Features Overview**

|**Feature**|**Brief Description**|
|---|---|
|**Auto-Generate PDF Invoices**|Creates invoices automatically when an order is placed or completed.|
|**Email Attachment System**|Sends invoices directly with WooCommerce order emails.|
|**Customizable Invoice Templates**|Modify layout, branding, colors, logos & invoice fields.|
|**Download & Print Options**|Admin & customers can download invoices from My Account / Orders.|
|**Proforma & Credit Notes**|Supports advanced documents like packing slips & refunds.|
|**Sequential Invoice Numbering**|Compliant with accounting & GST/tax rules.|
|**Bulk Invoice Generation**|Export multiple invoices for offline records or accounting.|
|**Multilingual & Multi-currency Ready**|Works with WPML, Polylang & international markets.|
|**Supports Tax / GST Fields**|Auto-populates VAT/GST fields based on checkout details.|
|**Integration with Accounting Tools**|Compatible with bookkeeping apps & ERP workflows.|
