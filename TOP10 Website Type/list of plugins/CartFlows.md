# **How to Approach : CartFlows (Sales Funnel Builder for WooCommerce)**

> **Senior Developer Guidance:**  
> **CartFlows** is a **sales funnel and checkout optimization plugin** built for WooCommerce.  
> It enables businesses to create **high-converting sales funnels, custom checkout pages, upsells/downsells, order bumps, thank-you funnels, and lead generation flows**â€”dramatically improving revenue per customer without extra traffic.

Use CartFlows when building **E-commerce stores, landing pages, course funnels, or paid booking flows**, and when **conversion optimization and post-purchase revenue growth** are priorities.
### **Features Overview**

|**Feature**|**Brief Description**|
|---|---|
|**Custom Checkout Page Builder**|Replace default WooCommerce checkout with optimized, UX-focused design.|
|**One-Click Upsells & Downsells**|Offer additional products after checkout to increase order value.|
|**Order Bump Offers**|Add impulse purchase options directly on the checkout page.|
|**Conversion-Optimized Funnel Templates**|Ready-made funnels for products, courses, events & services.|
|**A/B Split Testing**|Compare funnel versions & improve conversion performance.|
|**Lead Capture Funnels**|Create landing pages with opt-in forms and lead magnets.|
|**Thank-You Pages With Custom Data**|Personalized post-purchase experience & review request pages.|
|**Analytics & Funnel Insights**|Track sales performance & funnel behavior.|
|**Deep Page Builder Integration**|Works with Elementor, Bricks, Gutenberg & other builders.|
|**WooCommerce Compatibility**|Works with native products, coupons, payment gateways & shipping rules.|
