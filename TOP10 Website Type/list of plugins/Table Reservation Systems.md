# **How to Approach : Table Reservation Systems (Restaurant Booking & Seating Management)**

> **Senior Developer Guidance:**  
> **Table reservation plugins** allow restaurants and cafés to **accept online table bookings**, manage seating capacity, control schedules, automate email/SMS confirmations, and reduce manual reservation handling.  
> They integrate seamlessly with **restaurant websites, food ordering systems, and payment gateways**, giving customers a smooth booking experience while optimizing restaurant operations.

Use Table Reservation Systems when a restaurant website needs **booking calendars, reservation forms, confirmation automation, seating management, and shift control**.

### **Features Overview**

|**Feature**|**Brief Description**|
|---|---|
|**Online Booking Form**|Customers reserve tables directly from the website.|
|**Calendar & Seating Schedule**|Manage reservations by date, time, and seat capacity.|
|**Slot & Time Control**|Customize available shifts, duration, buffer time & max seats.|
|**Automatic Email / SMS Notifications**|Confirmation, reminders & cancellation alerts.|
|**Deposit / Payment Support**|Collect reservation fees via Stripe / PayPal / WooCommerce.|
|**Table Layout Management**|Assign table numbers, seating types & group size logic.|
|**Booking Restrictions**|Prevent overbooking & configure peak-hour limits.|
|**Customer Management Dashboard**|Track bookings, repeat customers & reservation history.|
|**Integration With Food Ordering**|Combine dine-in reservation + online ordering.|
|**Admin & Staff Access Roles**|Allow managers or staff to approve/modify bookings.|
