> **Senior Developer Guidance:**  
> **Bookly** is a **powerful automated booking and scheduling system** for WordPress, ideal for businesses that require **appointment booking, time-slot management, staff allocation, and online payments**.  
> It provides a seamless booking workflow for customers and automation tools for business owners â€” replacing manual scheduling with a fully digital system.

Use Bookly when a project needs **appointment handling**, **staff calendars**, **service scheduling**, or **automated notifications**, especially in service-based industries.
### **Features Overview**

|**Feature**|**Brief Description**|
|---|---|
|**Online Booking Form**|Customers can book appointments 24/7 through a modern multi-step form.|
|**Staff & Service Management**|Assign services and schedules to multiple employees.|
|**Real-Time Calendar System**|Sync with Google Calendar & manage availability.|
|**Online Payments Support**|Accept payments with PayPal, Stripe, WooCommerce & local gateways.|
|**Automated Email & SMS Reminders**|Reduce no-shows with notification workflows.|
|**Custom Time Slot & Duration Control**|Flexible booking rules for different service durations.|
|**Group Booking & Packages**|Sell sessions, classes, or bundled appointments.|
|**Customizable Form UI**|Edit booking steps to match branding and UX requirements.|
|**Multi-Language & Multi-Location Ready**|Works for businesses with multiple branches.|
|**Dashboard & Analytics**|Track bookings, revenue, and staff performance.|
