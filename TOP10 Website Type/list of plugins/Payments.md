# **How to Approach : Payment Gateways (Online Payments System)**

> **Senior Developer Guidance:**  
> **Payment gateways** enable businesses to **accept online payments securely** through Debit/Credit Cards, UPI, Net Banking, Wallets, and international payment methods.  
> They integrate with **WooCommerce** and booking/membership/LMS plugins to automate billing, renewals, invoices, subscriptions, and order processing.

Use Payment Gateways when the website needs **online checkout**, **service payments**, **course/booking purchase**, or **subscription billing**.

### **Features Overview**

|**Feature**|**Brief Description**|
|---|---|
|**UPI / Card / Net Banking / Wallet Support**|Enables multi-method digital payments for customers.|
|**WooCommerce Integration**|Seamlessly connects with store checkout pages for transactions.|
|**Recurring Subscription Billing**|Auto-renewal for memberships, coaching plans & LMS courses.|
|**Multi-Currency Support**|Accept payments globally (Stripe, PayPal).|
|**Order & Invoice Automation**|Auto-generate receipts and billing documents.|
|**Secure Encrypted Transactions**|PCI compliant SSL-based secure payment processing.|
|**Refund & Dispute Handling**|Supports partial/complete refunds and dispute management.|
|**Mobile-Friendly Checkout**|Smooth & responsive mobile checkout experience.|
|**Fraud & Risk Protection**|Advanced anti-fraud analytics for safe payments.|
|**Cart & Checkout Customization**|Modify checkout flows to increase conversion rates.|

### **Ideal Project Types**

- E-commerce stores
    
- Booking & appointment businesses
    
- LMS / online course selling websites
    
- Membership / subscription platforms
    
- Event & ticket booking websites
    
- Restaurants / food delivery & online ordering
