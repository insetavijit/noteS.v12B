# **How to Approach : Food Store (Online Food Ordering System for WooCommerce)**

> **Senior Developer Guidance:**  
> **Food Store** is a plugin built on top of **WooCommerce** that converts a WordPress website into a **food ordering and restaurant delivery system**, similar to **Swiggy / Zomato-style ordering experiences**.  
> It adds features like **menu management, add-ons, cart-based ordering, pickup & delivery scheduling**, and seamless integration with **WooCommerce payment gateways**.

Use Food Store when building websites for **restaurants, cafés, bakeries, cloud kitchens, catering services, or takeaway businesses** requiring **live ordering & customer checkout**.

### **Features Overview**

|**Feature**|**Brief Description**|
|---|---|
|**Online Food Ordering Menu**|Display food items with categories, pricing & variations.|
|**Add-ons / Custom Options**|Toppings, sizes, flavors & quantity-based add-ons.|
|**Pickup & Delivery Scheduling**|Customers choose delivery/pickup date & time slots.|
|**Real-time AJAX Cart System**|Order totals update without page reloads.|
|**Store Availability Control**|Opening/closing hours, holidays & custom schedule rules.|
|**Order Preparation & Status Control**|Notify customers about order confirmation, cooking, ready, delivered.|
|**Delivery Zones & Fees**|Define service areas & different delivery charges.|
|**WooCommerce Payment Integration**|Works with Stripe, RazorPay, COD, wallet payments etc.|
|**Responsive, App-like UI**|Mobile-friendly ordering experience.|
|**Shortcodes & Widget Support**|Integrate menu sections anywhere on site.|
