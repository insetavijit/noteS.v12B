### **Features Overview**

|**Feature**|**Brief Description**|
|---|---|
|**Modern Step-by-Step Booking Wizard**|Clean, intuitive and fast booking UI with minimal clicks.|
|**Staff & Service Scheduling**|Add employees, calendars, working hours, service durations & pricing.|
|**Customer Account System**|Users can login, manage bookings, update info and view history.|
|**Online Payment Support**|Stripe, PayPal and WooCommerce integration for paid bookings.|
|**SMS & Email Notifications**|Automated reminders and confirmations to reduce no-shows.|
|**Google Calendar Sync**|Real-time schedule synchronization.|
|**Powerful Admin Dashboard**|Visual insights for workload, performance & booking statistics.|
|**Coupons & Promotions**|Discount codes, special pricing and offers.|
|**Multi-location / Branch Support**|Works with multiple branches or franchises.|
|**Customizable Booking Fields**|Add custom questions and form fields based on services.|
