# **How to Approach : TutorLMS (Learning Management System)**

> **Senior Developer Guidance:**  
> **TutorLMS** is a full-featured **Learning Management System plugin** for WordPress, designed for creating and selling **online courses, training programs, and digital education platforms**.  
> It provides **course building, student dashboards, quizzes, assignments, certifications, and monetization tools**, making it ideal for coaches, academies, schools, and eLearning businesses.

Use TutorLMS when you need **course delivery, student tracking, and revenue systems** inside WordPress without relying on external platforms like Udemy or Teachable.
### **Features Overview**

|**Feature**|**Brief Description**|
|---|---|
|**Drag-and-Drop Course Builder**|Create lessons, topics, quizzes, video modules easily.|
|**Student & Instructor Dashboard**|Personal dashboards with progress tracking and notifications.|
|**Quiz & Assessment Tools**|Multiple question types, time limits, assignments & grading.|
|**Course Monetization**|Sell courses with WooCommerce / Stripe / PayPal / subscription systems.|
|**Course Prerequisites & Content Drip**|Control student flow and structured learning paths.|
|**Certificates & Completion Badges**|Auto-generate branded course completion certificates.|
|**Email Notifications & Communication**|Automated course and student activity alerts.|
|**Instructor Management System**|Multi-instructor platform with commission controls.|
|**Reviews & Rating System**|Social proof for better conversions.|
|**Reporting & Analytics**|Performance tracking for students, instructors & course revenue.|
