# **How to Approach : Documentation + Training + Post-Launch Support**

> **Senior Developer Guidance:**  
> A project is not complete when the website goes live ‚Äî **it is complete when the client is confident, empowered, and supported with clarity**.  
> Documentation ensures **long-term maintainability**, training ensures **usability**, and post-launch support ensures **trust & reliability**.  
> Professional delivery requires a **system**, not loose files or verbal instructions.

A well-structured handover elevates credibility, reduces support load, and transforms one-time projects into **long-term partnerships and recurring revenue**.

---

### **Topics Overview**

|Topic|Brief Description|
|---|---|
|**Project Documentation**|Setup details, credentials, workflows, update procedures|
|**Editing & CMS Training**|Video walkthroughs, admin tutorial guide|
|**Maintenance SOPs**|Backups, updates, cleanup, monitoring, roles|
|**Support & SLA Model**|Ticket system, response window, maintenance plans|
|**Performance & SEO Reporting**|Monthly reports, improvement roadmap|
|**Knowledgebase & Wiki**|Reusable help center for client education|
|**Versioning & Change Logs**|Transparent updates & history tracking|
|**End-of-project Handover**|Ownership transfer, deliverables, closing report|
